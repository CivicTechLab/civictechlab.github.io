Scholars from Canada, UK, US, Poland and Singapore co-organised a workshop on “design for online deliberation processes and technologies” during the 2015 CHI in Seoul, South Korea. This was the first time when the social scientists and computer scientists gathered in one room to tackle the challenge of online deliberation.
