[A forum on Internet and Media Use in Singapore’s General Election 2020](https://lkyspp.nus.edu.sg/ips/news/details/ips-online-forum-on-internet-and-media-use-in-general-election-2020) was held online on October 8th, 2020 and open to the public. The research team was led by Dr. Carol Soon from Institute of Policy Studies, NUS. The study was reported and covered in local media: [TodayOnline,](https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/ips-post-ge2020-forum-older-voters-catch-digital-use-parties-missed-crucial-online-signals) [Channel News Asia,](https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/ge2020-swing-voters-pap-opposition-party-ips-survey-13230088) [Mothership,](https://mothership.sg/2020/10/swing-voters-ge2020/) [Zaobao WeChat.](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/DSOOUADKhytd5c6ZfHTwHQ)
