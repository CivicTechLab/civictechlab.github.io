Deliberation and Decision Making (DDM2017): An Interdisciplinary Conference on Civic Tech was held in June, 2017 in Singapore. The conference led to the publication of a symposium “[Online Deliberation and #CivicTech](https://delibdemjournal.org/article/id/988/)” featured in the Journal of Deliberative Democracy.
