The CivicTech Lab has two interventions to make at [#CHI2026](https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/all/?keywords=%23chi2026\&origin=HASH_TAG_FROM_FEED). This year, along with long-term collaborators and new ones, we focus on exploring one question: what roles can [AI agents](https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/all/?keywords=%23aiagents\&origin=HASH_TAG_FROM_FEED) play in public [deliberation](https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/all/?keywords=%23deliberation\&origin=HASH_TAG_FROM_FEED)?

Panel:
"AI Agents and the Future of Deliberation: Designing Human–AI Collaboration for Democratic Dialogue." [Weiyu Zhang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/weiyuzhang/), [Simon Perrault](https://www.linkedin.com/in/simon-perrault-847049b5/), [Anna De Liddo](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anna-de-liddo-aa440729/), [Francesco Veri, PhD](https://www.linkedin.com/in/francescoveri/), [Maurice Flechtner](https://www.linkedin.com/in/maurice-flechtner/), [Horacio Saggion](https://www.linkedin.com/in/horacio-saggion-1749b916/), [Kaiyao Zhuang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kaiyao-zhuang-802924327/) will discuss various initiatives using AI in deliberation and organize a debate among the audiences. Time: Tue, 14 Apr | 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM; Venue: P1 - Room 117. More information here: [https://programs.sigchi.org/chi/2026/program/content/221865](https://programs.sigchi.org/chi/2026/program/content/221865)


Poster:
"Can AI Deliberate? Time: Wed, 15 Apr | 2:15 PM - 6:00 PM; Venue: Area 1 + 2 + 3: posters.
