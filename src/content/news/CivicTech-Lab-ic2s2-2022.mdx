The CivicTech Lab, in particular the team of Ms Huazhi Qin, Dr. Subhayan Mukerjee and Dr. Weiyu Zhang, presented at the 2022 International Conference on Computational Social Science ([ic2s2)](https://twitter.com/hashtag/ic2s2?src=hashtag_click) The study analyzes Facebook public pages’ posts on Covid-19 vaccines and found that topics from topic modeling can be seen as sub-issues in Networked Agenda Setting; approach vs withdrawal emotion may be better to understand sentiments’ impact on liking a post.
