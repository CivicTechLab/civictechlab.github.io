The CivicTech Lab is Going to ICA Annual Conference 2026!
4–8 June 2026 | Cape Town, South Africa

We are proud to contribute to [ICA26](https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/all/?keywords=%23ica26\&origin=HASH_TAG_FROM_FEED) through a keynote panel, an invited panel, and three paper presentations exploring online activism, digital environmentalism, platform cultures, and public discourse. Thanks go to our collaborators Prof. [Jun Liu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jun-liu-0a2a8522/), Prof. Hong Yu (Zhejiang U), Prof. [Yu Sun](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yu-sun-8a950071/), Prof. Lin Zhang (University of New Hampshire), Dr. Huailin Mu (Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University), Mr. Yongxu Lu (Chinese U of Science and Technology) and Dr. [Zhicong Chen](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jssyczc/).

Highlights from ICA 2026 include:

• Keynote Panel – “Whose Protest Counts? Power and Exclusion in Online Activism.”
• Preconference Paper – “Tracing the Evolution of Environmental Publics in Digital Environmentalism and Non-Confrontational Activism.”
• Invited Panel – “Mapping Platform Asia: A Collaborative Inquiry Into Global Digital Futures.”
• Paper – “The Intermediary Power of Transpositioning: How MCNs ‘Translate’ Cultural Content From Chinese Platforms to Overseas Platforms.”
• Paper – “Hyping Novel Food: Comparing Public Expectations on Facebook and Weibo.”

Grateful for the opportunity to exchange ideas and engage with scholars worldwide at [ICA2026](https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/all/?keywords=%23ica2026\&origin=HASH_TAG_FROM_FEED).
