Exciting news! 🎉 Under the leadership of Professor [Weibiao Zhou](https://www.linkedin.com/in/weibiao-zhou-79b823/) (Food Science and Technology, NUS), our research lab has been awarded the Singapore Food Story 2.0 Grant for the Social Science Package under “DIGEST: Developing Innovative Gastrointestinal Efficiency and Smart Technologies”. Professor [Weiyu Zhang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/weiyuzhang/) (CNM, NUS), Assistant Professor [Bo Pang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bo-pang-056b93206/) (FST, NUS) and Dean’s Chair Associate Professor [Alberto Salvo](https://www.linkedin.com/in/albertosalvo/) (Economics, NUS) will work closely to study the young population's understanding and acceptance of novel food products in Singapore.
